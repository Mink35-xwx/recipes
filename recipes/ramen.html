What is Ramen?

Ramen is a Japanese noodle soup featuring wheat noodles served in a flavorful broth, often topped with meat, vegetables, and seasonings.

Ramen Ingredients

This simple ramen recipe requires just a few ingredients:

  • Ramen Noodles: Instant ramen noodles work perfectly for this dish.
  • Vegetable Broth: Provides a flavorful base for the soup.
  • Soy Sauce: Adds a savory, umami taste.
  • Sesame Oil: Enhances the richness of the broth.
  • Chili Oil: Adds a touch of heat.
  • Ginger: Brings a warm, aromatic flavor.
  • Green Onions: Used as a fresh garnish.

How to Make Ramen

Making ramen at home is quick and easy. Follow these steps:

  1. Boil 3 ½ cups of vegetable broth in a large pot.
  2. Add 1 pack of instant ramen noodles (3.5 ounces) along with the dried vegetable packet.
  3. Stir and cook until the noodles are tender, about 3 minutes.
  4. Mix in 2 teaspoons of soy sauce, ½ teaspoon of chili oil, and ½ teaspoon of freshly minced ginger.
  5. Drizzle 1 teaspoon of sesame oil on top and garnish with 2 sliced green onions before serving.
